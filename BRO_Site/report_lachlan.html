Mission

The website has been designed with two goals in mind; firstly, it aimed to increase the number of attendees to the Barrier Reef Orchestra performances, and secondly aimed to lower the average age of attendees.
To this end, the website was built with the intent to be an advertising platform through which awareness of the Barrier Reef Orchestra could be spread, increasing general interest.
The new website was also to be more interactive and engaging. The objective was to make people spend more time on the website.
Finally, more than letting people know what the Barrier Reef Orchestra was doing and promoting their concerts, the website was aimed at becoming a source of information about classical music as well.
