Become a benefactor

With a philanthropic gift of $200 or more, you can become an Orchestra Benefactor and be placed on our Benefactor roll for 12 months.

This roll will be published in each concert program and on our website for 12 months.

As a benefactor, you will also receive 2 complimentary tickets to our next concert, be placed on the mailing list for our newsletter and will be a guest at our annual Season's Launch function.

Find here the Benefactors' roll (7th August 2015)

The North Queensland Ensembles Inc is a tax deductible fund listed on the Register of Cultural Organisations under Subdivision 30-B of the Income tax Assessment Act 1997. Donations of $2.00 or more are tax deductible.
